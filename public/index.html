Session Management
Current Session
Your bot is linked to a WhatsApp account via a saved session. The session is stored securely on the server.
⚠️ Re-link to New Number
This will delete the current session and restart the bot. You'll need to scan a new QR code to link a different WhatsApp number.
How to re-link:
1. Click "Clear Session" above
2. Wait ~10 seconds for QR to appear
3. Scan with WhatsApp on your phone
4. Bot reconnects automatically ✅
Deploy to Railway — SESSION_ID
Railway buang session files setiap kali deploy. Gunakan SESSION_ID supaya bot boleh connect tanpa scan QR semula.

Cara guna:
1. Pastikan bot Connected di sini dulu
2. Klik Export SESSION_ID di bawah
3. Copy nilai yang keluar
4. Pergi Railway → Service → Variables → tambah SESSION_ID = nilai tadi
5. Redeploy Railway — bot akan connect terus tanpa QR ✅
